YoIntern

Developer

As a pivotal member of YoIntern's development team, my tasks are instrumental in shaping the foundation of our innovative internship job board platform. With YoIntern poised as a dynamic startup on the brink of its launch, my responsibilities encompass a spectrum of crucial tasks aimed at ensuring a seamless user experience and platform functionality.

At the forefront of my tasks is the design and development of user interfaces using React. These interfaces serve as the gateway for interns and employers to interact with our platform. By crafting intuitive and visually appealing interfaces, I aim to create an engaging experience that facilitates effortless navigation and enhances user satisfaction.

In tandem with interface design, I am entrusted with architecting and managing our database infrastructure using PostgreSQL. This involves the meticulous design of database schemas tailored to accommodate the diverse needs of our users while prioritizing data integrity and scalability. By implementing efficient data storage solutions, I lay the groundwork for seamless data management and retrieval, enabling our platform to deliver timely and relevant information to users.
